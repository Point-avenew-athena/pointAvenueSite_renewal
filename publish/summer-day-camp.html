What's Awating Future Global Citizens?

  • A new standard in summer education with a dedication to excellence, focusing on 3 pillars: Academic, Physical and Character Development
  • Innovative, challenging, and enriching programs that span from English skills, Debate, Life Coaching, and Test Preparation designed to motivate, engage your child, and prepared for long-term success.
  • Taught by experienced and internationally qualified mentors.
  • A mentorship philosophy and a multicultural environment that empowers young students to mature into disciplined, independent, and responsible people.
  • Small class size (max 8 students for elementary class), with a fun, engaging, and creative environment. Students will have many opportunities for one-on-one mentorship.
