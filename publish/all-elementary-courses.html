ELEMENTARY COURSES

The Elementary English Learning program is designed to develop students’ English literacy development skills from pre-kindergarten to grade 4 level. The program focuses on building four critical blocks of language: Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking. The contents of this course bridge ESL methodology and components of the US Common Core State Standards to ensure our students can be successful at home and as global citizens. More importantly, we are committed to promoting the 21st Century Skills that will help our students thrive through the integration of character, critical thinking, collaboration, communication, and creativity. Our Elementary curriculum and teaching methodology encourage the use of cooperative, and inquiry-based learning and enriches our students’ academic, social, and emotional skills.
